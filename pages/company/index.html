Our Mission

Unlocking the potential of data requires Smart People.

We empower analysts, engineers, product managers, marketers, operations and other specialists to use digital event data to do transformative things with this data.

When the world wide web was born in 1989, the web was a much simpler place than it is today.

What you could do on the web was limited to reading documents and clicking hyperlinks. And the corresponding web analytics data generated was not so interesting.

Digital event data today is fascinating, detailed, behavioural data that describes the actions and journeys of billions of individuals engaging in all aspects of their lives across the world. We, the data community, have only scratched the surface in terms of what is possible with all that data.

For companies engaging with users through digital platforms, this data is potentially invaluable. Used right, the data can enable those companies to better understand and better serve those customers. That data can be used to ask and answer an impossibly large set of fascinating questions.

Used right, the insight gleaned is transformative: enabling companies to develop deep user-level understanding and use that to delight their customers, identify and execute on new opportunities, to grow and to flourish.

Take control of your data. Realize data possibility.

Our mission at Snowplow is to empower analysts, engineers, product managers, marketers, operations and other specialists to use digital event data to do transformative things with this data.

We are open. Digital event data is so vast, and the range of questions and uses that data can be put to so enormous, that any vendor who thinks they can meet all those needs is both arrogant and stupid.

At Snowplow, we build technology to unlock the value of data. Part of that is ensuring that our users have unfettered ownership and access to their own data, in a structured, easy to use format, so that they can plugin as many different analysis tools as possible. The data space is full of companies building innovative tools. We want our users to be able to work with any of them: a decision to work with Snowplow should never be at the expense of the myriad other services out there.

The world of data opportunity is much too big for one vendor, or even a handful of big vendors.
