Use data to drive understanding,
and understanding to drive impact.

Snowplow empower analysts, engineers, product managers, marketers, operations and other specialists to use data to do transformative things.

When the web was born in 1989, it was a simple place. You could load documents and click links. Web data was not so interesting.

Today our whole lives are mediated through digital platforms. We fall in love, play, gamble, form friendships, keep fit, manage our finances, work, arrange transport, shop, eat and more, all via apps and websites.

We, the data community, have only scratched the surface in terms of what is possible with all that data.

Digital event data today is detailed, behavioural data that describes the actions and journeys of billions of individuals engaging in all aspects of their lives across the world.

For companies engaging with users through
digital platforms, this data is potentially
transformative.

Used right, the data can enable better user understand.
That can be used to develop better products and services, that surprise and delight users.
